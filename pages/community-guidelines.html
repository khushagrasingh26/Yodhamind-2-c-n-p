Community Guidelines

YodhaMind's student community is built on empathy, support, and strict moderation. Our goal is to create a safe space for every Indian student to feel heard, seen, and supported without fear of judgment.

🛡️ The Golden Rules

  • 100% Anonymity: Do not post personally identifiable information (PII) like full names, phone numbers, email addresses, or exact locations.
  • Zero Tolerance for Bullying: Name-calling, discrimination, hate speech, or targeted harassment will result in an immediate ban.
  • Empathy First: Be kind. If you cannot offer constructive support or a listening ear, scroll past.

🚨 Safety & Crisis

  • No Harmful Content: Posts promoting self-harm, suicide, or violence against others are strictly prohibited. The system automatically detects and intercepts distress keywords to connect the user with immediate professional help.
  • Medical Disclaimer: This community is not a substitute for professional therapy or medical advice. Please consult a counselor or call emergency services if you are in a crisis.

✨ Moderation Process

  • Active Review: Every post is actively reviewed. Content violating guidelines will be removed within 2 hours.
  • Reporting: Users can report posts they believe violate guidelines. Moderation teams review reports continuously.
